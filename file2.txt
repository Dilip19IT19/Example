Pizza is a popular Italian dish that has become a worldwide favorite. It traditionally consists of a freshly-baked crust topped with tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, and various toppings such as vegetables, meats, and herbs. The pizza can be baked in a wood-fired oven, brick oven, or even on a grill. The crust can also be made thin and crispy or thick and fluffy, depending on personal preference. Pizza is a versatile dish that can be enjoyed in many different ways, whether as a full meal, a snack, or a party food.
